Data Confinement

Central collection of personal data can be a threat since an attacker gaining access to a user's account can basically access all their data.

Data Store

When stored by third parties data subjects lose control over their documents.

Metadata

Metadata are a way to describe data. So technically metadata are data about data.

Tracking Protection

Websites collect an incredible amount of information about their visitors via attributes of the browser. This information is shared with third parties and saved in logs.
